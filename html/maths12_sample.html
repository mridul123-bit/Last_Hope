Benefits of Solving the Last Years CBSE Board Class 12 Maths Papers

There are various advantages of solving the last 10 years CBSE board papers. Here we have listed few them:

1.Students get well versed with the exam pattern and question paper pattern.

2.They get to know the types of questions asked in the exam and their difficulty level.

3.Solving these papers will help them in analyzing their current exam preparation.

4.Students can find out their strong and weak areas after practising these papers.

5.They will feel more confident to write the paper.
